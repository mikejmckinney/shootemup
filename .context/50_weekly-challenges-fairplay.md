# Weekly Challenges & Fair Play (Authoritative)

## Purpose
Skill-based competitive mode that remains fair regardless of progression.

## Standardization
- Fixed ship + fixed starting loadout
- Normalized stats (damage, HP, speed)
- Fixed RNG seed per weekly challenge

## Scoring
- Score based on performance: survival time, objectives completed, combo efficiency
- Optional modifiers: higher risk yields higher scoring potential

## Leaderboards
- Global + friends
- Anti-cheese: detect AFK patterns, cap exploit loops
- Tie-breakers: time, damage taken, objective precision
