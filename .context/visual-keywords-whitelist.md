# Visual Keywords Whitelist

Use these terms and concepts when generating visuals for this project.

## Rendering & Style
- HD 2D
- Vector-style illustration
- Clean geometry
- Smooth edges
- Flat color surfaces
- Subtle gradients
- Modern arcade aesthetic
- Premium indie game look

---

## Composition
- Strong focal point
- Clear silhouette separation
- Balanced negative space
- Gameplay-first framing
- Readable at a glance

---

## Color & Light
- Neon accents
- Controlled glow
- High contrast
- Dark background, bright gameplay
- Color-coded systems

---

## Sci-Fi Aesthetic
- Futuristic but grounded
- Clean technology
- Energy-based weapons
- Abstract space environments
- Stylized sci-fi, not realism

---

## UI Language
- Minimalist HUD
- Floating panels
- Icon-driven UI
- Clean sci-fi dashboards
- Competitive readability

---

## Emotional Tone
- Confident
- Precise
- High-energy but controlled
- Skill-driven
- Modern arcade intensity
