## Composition Notes

## Prompt
Banner key art for modern arcade shooter. HD 2D vector-style illustration, clean geometric shapes, flat color surfaces with subtle gradients, smooth edges, modern arcade sci-fi aesthetic. Top-down view. Sharp angular player ship with cyan glow on left side, projectile streams flowing right. Enemy wave approaching from right with red/orange identity. Deep navy to black gradient background. Minimal background detail. Strong separation between gameplay elements. Clean geometric placeholder rectangle in upper right for logo. Controlled glow effects. Readable at a glance. Premium confident presentation.
