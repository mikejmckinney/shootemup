## Purpose
End-of-run results screen showing score breakdown and rewards earned.

## Composition Notes
Central panel with score at top. Breakdown items listed. Rewards/unlocks shown with icons. Play again as primary action.

## Prompt
Results screen UI mockup for modern arcade shooter. HD 2D vector-style illustration, clean geometric shapes, flat color surfaces with subtle gradients, smooth edges. Large central floating panel. Final score prominently displayed at top with large numerals. Score breakdown: survival time, objectives, combo efficiency icons with values. Rewards section: currency earned (gold icon), XP gained, potential unlock indicator. Primary action button: Play Again. Secondary: Main Menu. Dark background with subtle celebration glow. Icon-first presentation. Geometric sans typography feel. Minimalist sci-fi panel. Premium confident layout.

## Negative Prompt
pixel art, retro style, texture noise, cluttered breakdown, text-heavy, particle confetti spam, decorative frames, small text, realistic, painterly

## Consistency Constraints
- UI panel style: large central floating panel
- Icon style: breakdown icons, reward icons
- Color palette: dark background, gold for rewards
- Typography style: large numerals for score, geometric sans feel
