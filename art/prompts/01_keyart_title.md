## Purpose
Primary title/key art featuring the player ship in dynamic combat pose with clean logo space.

## Composition Notes
Center-weighted composition with player ship as hero element. Enemy silhouettes recede into background. Clear negative space in upper third for title placement. Strong diagonal energy lines.

## Prompt
Key art illustration for modern arcade shooter game. HD 2D vector-style illustration, clean geometric shapes, flat color surfaces with subtle gradients, smooth edges, modern arcade sci-fi aesthetic, premium confident presentation. Top-down perspective. Angular arrow-like player ship with cyan/blue identity, centered and dynamic, firing bright projectiles. Multiple enemy silhouettes in red/orange receding into deep navy background. Controlled neon glow effects. Clear readable silhouettes. Balanced negative space in upper portion for logo. High contrast between player, enemies, and dark background. Strong focal point on player ship. Premium mobile/PC arcade feel.

pixel art, retro style, texture noise, grit, film grain, busy background, fog, smoke, realistic, painterly, blurry edges, cluttered composition, low contrast, muddy colors, text, logos, watermarks

## Consistency Constraints

- Enemy shapes follow established red/orange identity
