## Purpose
Player ship asset sheet showing 4-6 distinct silhouettes with trait indicators.

## Composition Notes
Grid layout with ships on neutral dark background. Each ship shows front view and trait icon. Strong silhouette differences. Consistent cyan/blue identity.

## Prompt
Player ship asset sheet for modern arcade shooter. HD 2D vector-style illustration, clean geometric shapes, flat color surfaces with subtle gradients, smooth edges. Grid of 5 distinct player ships on dark navy background. Each ship: angular forward-facing silhouette with cyan/blue identity color, unique shape variation (arrow-like, wider wings, sleek needle, bulky armored, asymmetric scout). Small trait icon below each ship. Strong silhouette differences visible. Profile recognizable at small scale. Consistent rendering style across all ships. Clean presentation. No overlapping elements. Asset sheet format.

## Negative Prompt
pixel art, retro style, texture noise, identical silhouettes, cluttered, overlapping ships, realistic, painterly, grit, busy background

## Consistency Constraints
- Player ship silhouette: all share angular forward-motion feel, but distinct variations
- Color palette: cyan/blue identity for all player ships
- Shape language: arrow-like, wider wings, sleek needle, bulky armored, asymmetric scout
